Projects

This is where I'll talk about academic or professional projects I am working on or have completed.

Modeling Blood Flow With Differential Equations

To complete an "honors petition", just a way to gain honors credit for a regular class, I worked with my Differential Equations professor on a project in which I modify a simple model of the human cardiovascular system, and plot numerical results. I then wrote up a qualitative analysis on the findings, and properties of the model.

Tools/Technologies: MATLAB, LaTeX

Using MATLAB to Reccomend Restaurants

This was an assignment in a computing for engineers course involving taking data from an open-source dataset, and performing numerical calculations on location data to return a nearby restaurant and its information.

Tools/Technologies: Python, Flask, SQL
