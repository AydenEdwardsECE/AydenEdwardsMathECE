About Me

I am an Honors Mathematics at the University of Houston.

Since I haven't learned enough math to decide what I want to specialize in, I am taking as many high-level courses as I can. Right now I'm leaning towards some area of geometry, algebraic or differential. I have course experience in Linear Algebra, low-level Numerical Analysis, and Intermediate Analysis.

I have been selected as president of "Cougar Mathletics" at UH, (functionally it's the UH Math Club). I love problem solving leisurely and hope to compete in the Putnam Competition in December. I'm also involved in Honors Ambassadors at UH, IEEE UH, and Code[Coogs].

I have worked as a tutor at San Jacinto College since high school, and this August I will have worked there for 2 years. I have been a private tutor for a little over a year now.

Outside of math, I love playing soccer and watching Basketball. Catch me at every home basketball game at UH this season.Go Coogs!
